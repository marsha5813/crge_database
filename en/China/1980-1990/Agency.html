  1. Original Denominations after Sectarianism 1982 (Zheng Jianye 1983: 11; Starr 2015: 2045-2057; Chan 2019: 86-91)

The reconstruction of the Chinese church after the Cultural Revolution mainly referred to the Three-Self system established after the 1958 united worship, which excluded the possibility of restoring denominational organizations.
